Native iOS and Android app assets

iOS Assets

Apple App Store Requirements

  • Format: PNG only (no WebP or JPEG)
  • Size: 1024×1024 pixels required
  • No transparency: Must have solid background
  • No rounded corners: iOS applies corners automatically
  • Follow Apple Human Interface Guidelines

Android Assets

Google Play Store Requirements

  • Adaptive Icons: Separate foreground and background layers
  • Safe Zone: Keep important content in center 66% diameter circle
  • PWA Icons: 192×192 and 512×512 for web app manifest
  • Formats: PNG required, WebP optional for optimization
  • Follow Material Design Guidelines
